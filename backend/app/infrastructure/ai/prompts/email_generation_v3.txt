Rules for subject:
- If job_title is known AND company is known: "Application for [job_title] at [Company]".
- If job_title is known but company is unknown: "Application for [job_title]" (no "at" suffix, no placeholder).
- If multiple roles are listed, pick the ONE from the post that best matches the candidate's resume skills. Never invent a role not listed in the post.
- If neither job_title nor company is known: "Job Application".
- Keep under 80 characters.
- Never write "at your company", "at [Company]", or any literal placeholder — omit the company portion entirely when it is not known.

Rules for body:
- Plain text only, no HTML or markdown.
- Use blank lines (double newline) between every paragraph — this is critical for readability.
- Opening line: always use "Dear Hiring Manager," — never use the recruiter_name in the greeting.
- Follow with a short warm line, e.g. "I hope you are doing well."
- Paragraph 1: State the specific role you are applying for. Do not mention where or how you found the listing.
- Paragraph 2: Highlight the candidate's most relevant skills and tech stack from the resume, specific to this role. Be concrete — name technologies, years of experience, and measurable impact where available.
- Paragraph 3: Mention 1-2 specific projects from the resume that are most relevant to this role. Name them explicitly and describe what they do in one sentence each.
- Paragraph 4: One sentence on why this company or role is exciting to the candidate.
- Closing line: "I would appreciate the opportunity to discuss my application further. Thank you for your time and consideration."
- Profile details section: If $candidate_profile_section is non-empty, copy the exact bullet lines from it and insert them immediately before the signature block (no heading). If $candidate_profile_section is empty or blank, skip this section entirely — do NOT add any profile bullet points.
- Signature block (each item on its own line, no blank lines within the block):
    Best Regards,
    $candidate_name
    [Phone number from resume if present]
    [Email from resume if present]
    [Include only if non-empty: $linkedin_url]
    [Include only if non-empty: $github_url]
    [Include only if non-empty: $website_url]
- Do not mention match score or missing skills.
- Do not fabricate experience — only reference what is in the resume.
- Total word count: 180-300 words.
