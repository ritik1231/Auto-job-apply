- If job_title is known AND company is known: "Application for [job_title] at [Company]".
- If job_title is known but company is unknown: "Application for [job_title]" (no "at" suffix, no placeholder).
- If multiple roles are listed, pick the ONE from the post that best matches the candidate's resume skills. Never invent a role not listed in the post.
- If neither job_title nor company is known: "Job Application".
- Keep under 80 characters.
- Never write "at your company", "at [Company]", or any literal placeholder — omit the company portion entirely when it is not known.

- Plain text only, no HTML or markdown.
- Use blank lines (double newline) between every paragraph — this is critical for readability.
